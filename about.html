The Story Behind Studio Foam

An independent app studio with a focus on quality over quantity.

Why "Foam"?

Foam is structured chaos. Look closely at any foam—whether it's the head on your coffee, the inside of a crash pad, or the texture of a memory foam pillow—and you'll see an intricate network of cells, each one unique yet part of a cohesive whole.

That's how we think about software. Every feature, every screen, every interaction is its own little cell with a purpose. But together, they form something greater—something that adapts to pressure, cushions the impact, and supports what matters.

Plus, foam is lightweight. And we really like lightweight apps.

The Philosophy

We believe in building software that respects your time and attention. That means:

  • No bloat. Every feature earns its place. If it doesn't make the app meaningfully better, it doesn't ship.
  • No dark patterns. We don't trick you into subscriptions, spam you with notifications, or design for addiction.
  • No surveillance capitalism. Your data is yours. We collect the minimum needed to make the app work, and that's it.
  • Focused purpose. Each app does one thing exceptionally well, rather than doing everything poorly.

Studio Foam is a one-person operation. I'm a developer and climber who got frustrated with the state of training apps. Most workout apps don't understand that climbing training is different—it's not just sets and reps, it's time under tension, specific grip positions, and structured protocols that require precise timing.

So I built Flash Forward for myself first. Then I realized other climbers might want it too. And here we are.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me at the climbing gym working on my weaknesses (it's always slopers), brewing coffee, or thinking about the next app idea.
